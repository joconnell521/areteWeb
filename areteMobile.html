Arete

From the Greek : The act of living up to one's potential. An app for electronic journaling that's personal and involves working towards small goals in all aspects of one's life. Research shows that just working towards goals increases a person's sense of well-being. In addition, the app provides reminders and reinforcement. This serves to MOTIVATE which involves a release of dopamine in the brain, a neurotransmitter associated with happiness. Also, the app provides affirmation which stimulates the REWARD CENTER of the brain and you also get a release of dopamine. It is time to save a generation (or two) and get on board with Arete!
